TomaTech is a mobile app that enables farmers and gardeners to detect tomato diseases confidently. Utilizing advanced machine learning, it provides real-time analysis and actionable solutions to enhance crop quality and yield. With an intuitive design, TomaTech fosters collaboration and integrates traditional farming with modern agri-tech, helping users increase productivity and reduce losses.

About Us

With TomaTech, our mission is to empower tomato farmers with innovative technology that enhances disease detection and improves crop productivity. Many new farmers face challenges in identifying diseases that threaten their tomato plants, leading to significant crop losses and reduced yields.

We are proud to introduce TomaTech: Your Smart Companion for Tomato Disease Detection. Our intuitive mobile application allows farmers to quickly and accurately identify diseases by leveraging our expertise in machine learning and cloud computing. With a user-friendly interface, TomaTech is designed for farmers of all experience levels, helping them improve the quality and quantity of their tomato crops through real-time insights and precise disease identification.

FAQ

TomaTech is a mobile application designed to help farmers quickly and accurately detect diseases in tomato plants. It uses advanced machine learning technology to identify symptoms and provide solutions, improving crop health and productivity.

TomaTech works by analyzing images of your tomato plants through the app. The machine learning model compares the images to a database of known diseases and provides a diagnosis along with advice on treatment and prevention.

Currently, TomaTech is available for Android devices. We are working on expanding the app to iOS in the future.

TomaTech’s disease detection accuracy is based on a custom-trained machine learning model, which has been tested and refined for optimal results. While it performs well in detecting common diseases, we recommend double-checking with a local agricultural expert for confirmation in critical cases.

Currently, TomaTech is focused on tomato plant diseases. However, we are working on expanding the app’s capabilities to support other crops in the future.
